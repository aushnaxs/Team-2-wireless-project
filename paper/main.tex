\section{Conclusion}
This research paper analyzes the current techniques used to improve energy efficiency and reduce computational power, along with environmental costs. Although optimization protocols reduce resource usage, it is the training and deployment of large-scale AI models that require high demands. To address this issue, an energy-aware AI framework is proposed to limit reliance on complex models by using lightweight systems and selectively using advanced models. Overall, designing sustainable networking designs requires maintaining a balance between optimization and energy awareness. Further research is needed to focus on strategic and efficient deployment of AI models rather than increasing their complexity.
