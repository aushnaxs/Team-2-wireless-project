## 1.  Summary (3-5 sentences in your own words)
This paper discusses the potential for digital twin systems to support the development of autonomous AI-driven 6G radio access networks by simulating real-world network conditions in real-time. The AI models will be able to predict and improve the network's efficiency by simulating the real-world network conditions virtually with the help of the digital twin systems. However, the simulation environment is a resource-intensive process.
