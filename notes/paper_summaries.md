## Paper Summary: AI-DRIVEN NETWORK OPTIMIZATION FOR
5G AND BEYOND

Authors:R. Sekkappan
Year: 2024

### 1. Problem
As mobile (5G/6G) grows they become to complex for individuals to manage. Traditional systems are static 
(they are always powered on until the system is not needed), which is a massive energy waste. 

### 2. Approach
The author approach to solve this problem was an “AI Brain” for the network. Instead of fixed rules, the system 
will combine machine learning and statistics to predivt when individuals will use the network, 
learn the most efficient way to send data and auto adjust power levels in real time. 

### 3. Key Findings
1.	Significant reduction in the need for operational staff and manual intervention. 
2.	AI implementation is projected to save global operators $300 billion annually. 
It also notes a 23% reduction in network operational expenses and a 42.8% reduction in maintenance expense
3.	52% reduction in energy consumption through the use of intelligent resource management and 
predictive load balancing


### 4. Relevance to Our Topic
This article relates to sustainability paradox, ai will improve the green networking by turning 
off unnecessary power.
