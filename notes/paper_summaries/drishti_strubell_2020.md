# Paper Summary

## Paper Title: : Energy and Policy Considerations for Modern Deep Learning Research
## Authors: Strubell, E., Ganesh, A., & McCallum, A.
## Year: 2020

**Full Citation :** Strubell, E., Ganesh, A., & McCallum, A. (2020). Energy and Policy Considerations for Modern Deep Learning Research. Proceedings of the AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence, 34(09), 13693–13696. https://doi.org/10.1609/aaai.v34i09.7123
## 1. Summary (3-5 sentences in your own words)

The paper discusses the environmental and financial implications associated with training large deep learning models such as BERT and GPT-2. The authors calculate the electricity consumed during training and additional data center cooling requirements. The results show that training modern AI models results in significant carbon emissions and requires extremely high financial investments, especially when multiple experiments are required for small accuracy improvements. The paper also emphasizes the significance of efficient hardware and research for the environmental impact of AI development.

## 2. Relevance to Our Survey

The relevance of the paper to our survey lies in the fact that it discusses the energy consumption by AI systems, which relates to the sustainability aspect in AI-driven wireless network optimization and telecom systems.

## 3. Critical Comment

The paper clearly emphasizes the environmental impact associated with training large AI models. The estimates are also accurate for the hardware settings, but the results may vary for modern energy-efficient hardware.
