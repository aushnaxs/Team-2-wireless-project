## 1. Summary (3-5 sentences in your own words)
The authors of this paper aim to study the environmental and financial consequences of training large-scale deep learning models like BERT and GPT-2. They calculated the amount of electricity required for training these models, including the cost of data center cooling. The authors concluded that training these models results in a high amount of carbon emissions and financial expenses, especially when repeated experiments are performed to achieve marginal improvements. This paper emphasizes the need for efficient hardware and eco-friendly approaches to minimize the environmental consequences of developing these models.
